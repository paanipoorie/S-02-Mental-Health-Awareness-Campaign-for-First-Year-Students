> CUSoC Project Submission
> **S-02: Mental Health Awareness Campaign for First-Year Students**

---

## 📌 Overview

The transition into university is one of the most challenging periods in a student's life. Many first-year students experience anxiety, homesickness, academic stress, loneliness, and uncertainty while remaining unaware of available support systems.

This project aims to design, implement, and evaluate a structured mental health awareness campaign that improves awareness, reduces stigma, and encourages help-seeking behavior among first-year undergraduate students.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Assess baseline mental health awareness among first-year students.
* Measure knowledge of available mental health resources.
* Reduce stigma associated with seeking psychological support.
* Conduct peer-led awareness workshops.
* Create an accessible mental health resource map.
* Evaluate campaign impact using pre/post surveys.
* Build a reusable toolkit for future student organizers.
